Professional Sports

Indiana Pacers

Go for a professional NBA game at Gainbridge Fieldhouse in downtown Indianapolis. Watch as the best basketball players in Indiana compete against other teams in the country.

Indianapolis Colts

Enjoy watching the former Super Bowl NFL team the Indianapolis Colts as they take on other professional football teams at Lucas Oil Stadium. Located in downtown Indianapolis its a site of the city.

Motor Speedway

Hosting many events a year including the world famous Indianapolis 500, the Indianapolis motor Speedway is a true spectacle of racing.
